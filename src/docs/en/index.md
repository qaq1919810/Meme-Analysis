# Welcome to the Meme Guide

Welcome to this project! This is a specifically compiled "Guide to Explaining the Humor" for videos, designed to help you quickly understand the memes and humorous points within them.

## Project Description
- This document provides detailed explanations of the video's humor points, the origin of the memes, and relevant background information.
- It is suitable for those who want to quickly grasp the memes or review the humorous content in the videos.

## Notes
- Each document is organized according to the video sequence.
- If you encounter a meme you don't understand, you can use the left-hand navigation to quickly find the relevant explanation.
- Suggestions for new memes or improvements are welcome to make the guide even better.

## Basic Concepts
1. Homophonic Pun: A native-language word is heard/interpreted as another native-language word.
2. Mondegreen (Air-Hearing): A foreign-language sound is heard/interpreted as a native-language word.
3. Reverse Mondegreen: A native-language sound is heard/interpreted as a foreign-language word.
4. Mondegreen Pun: Includes concepts 1 and 2.
5. Reverse Mondegreen Pun: Includes concepts 1 and 3.
6. Visual Similarity: One object or thing is perceived as another object or thing due to similar appearance.
