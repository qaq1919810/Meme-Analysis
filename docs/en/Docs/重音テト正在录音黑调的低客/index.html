    • 低调的黑客

      [00:00] BiliBili YouTube The term "低调的黑客" (Dīdiào de Hēikè - The Low-Key Hacker) comes from a new meme that emerged around October 2025. These memes usually start with "Do you know how terrifying an angry hacker is?" and are typically paired with some nonsensical, irrelevant video footage.

    • 低客的黑调

      [00:11] BiliBili YouTube
      It's a rearrangement of "低调的黑客" into "低客的黑调" (Dī kè de hēi diào). Its Hanyu Pinyin is "Dī kè de hēi diào," which simultaneously acts as a Reverse Mondegreen Pun for "dick's 黑屌" (hēi diǎo - black penis).

    • 看完blue的是这个

      [00:16] BiliBili YouTube
      "blue" is a Reverse Mondegreen for "不撸" (bù lǔ). The actual meaning of the phrase is "The one who doesn't '撸' (lǔ) after watching is this," where "撸" refers to male masturbation.

    • 注意图中没有领带

      [00:19] BiliBili YouTube
      The tie (领带) is visually associated with the male reproductive organ (phallus).

    • 注意黑客不一定是低调，而黑调一定是低客

      [00:23] BiliBili YouTube
      The first half of the sentence sets up the appearance of the second half. "而黑调一定是低客" (And 黑调 must be 低客) is a Reverse Mondegreen for "而黑屌一定是dick" (And black penis must be a dick).

    • 你现在一定在看b站，你一定看到了这视频，你一定看到了tietou

      [00:30] BiliBili YouTube
    • "b站" is the common Chinese abbreviation for the video platform BiliBili. The source video is on BiliBili.
    • "tietou" is the phonetic transcription of "Teto" from "重音teto" (Kasane Teto).
      Therefore, this sentence is an example of [Wastewater Literature (废话文学)] (stating the obvious).

    • 91-78=13

      [00:36] BiliBili YouTube
    • "91" here refers to "91传媒/91制片厂," organizations that produce explicit videos/content.
    • "78" is a Homophonic Pun for "鸡巴" (jība - penis).
    • "13" here is visually similar to "B," which is a Homophonic Pun for "逼" (bī - female genitalia).

    • 青春伤痛一定是遗憾，低调的黑客看过魔丸

      [00:36] BiliBili YouTube
    • "青春伤痛一定是遗憾" (Youth pain must be regret) ostensibly talks about regret but implicitly suggests that youth pain might stem from sexual experiences.
    • "低调的黑客" is meaningless here. "魔丸" (Mówán - Demon Pill/Orb) is a popular nickname for the protagonist "哪吒" (Nezha) in the Chinese film 哪吒之魔童闹海. (Note: Linked to the 2019 film based on the context, as that is the common "Mó Wán" reference).

    • Partial Word Definitions:

      • 购袋来了 (Gòudài lái le)

        "购袋" (Gòudài - Shopping Bag) refers to the gender "Walmart shopping bag." It's a popular meme that originated on English-speaking internet forums and social media (like Reddit, Tumblr, etc.), often used as a playful, sarcastic, or mocking commentary on identity politics and non-binary gender identities.

      • 武机来了 (Wǔjī lái le)

        "武机" (Wǔjī - Attack Helicopter) refers to the gender "attack helicopter." Same as above.

      • 哈基米南，北绿豆哦 (Hajimīnán, Běi lǜ dòu ō)

        "哈基米南北绿豆" (Hajimīnán Běi lǜ dòu) is a Mondegreen for the Japanese phrase "はちみつなめると" (hachimitsu nameru to, meaning "If you lick honey..."). "はちみつ" (Hachimitsu - Honey) is from the scene in Umamusume Pretty Derby Season 2 around 10 minutes and 20 seconds of episode 12, where the character Tokai Teio hums it while going to buy a honey drink. The phrase "なめると" (Nameru to) is transcribed as "南北绿豆" (Nán běi lǜ dòu - South North Mung Bean) because "哈基米南北绿豆" has recently been frequently used by creators to fill lyrics for various songs.

      • 350234，往日种种，可有话说，再无话说，苦命鸳鸯

      • 350234 is the number of a work on 18Comic. This work became famous due to its bizarre plot, which is roughly about the love story between Lu Bu and Dong Zhuo.
      • "往日种种，可有话说，再无话说，苦命鸳鸯" (All the past, anything to say? nothing more to say, ill-fated couple) are lines of dialogue from some images in 350234.

      • 前面忘了，中间忘了，后面忘了 (Forgot the front, forgot the middle, forgot the end)

        This phrase is usually used as a perfunctory way to answer a question before stating an opinion.
        For example: Forgot the front, forgot the middle, forgot the end, in short "苦命鸳鸯".
        The general idea is that regardless of the context, the situation is similar to that of an "苦命鸳鸯" (ill-fated couple) scene.

    • 我是奶龙，我才是奶龙 (I am 奶龙, I am the real 奶龙)

      [01:19] BiliBili YouTube
      "奶龙" (Nǎilóng - Milk Dragon) is a fictional dinosaur from the children's animation series 奶龙 and its spin-offs. It became popular because of its somewhat bizarre behavior, which is visually similar to Down syndrome.
      "我是奶龙，我才是奶龙" comes from the scene of the true and false "奶龙" in Episode 126 of 奶龙.

    • 注意图中没有领带 (Note there is no tie in the picture).
    • In "注意图中没有白色" (Note there is no white in the picture), the color white (白色) is visually associated with male semen (精液).

      [01:31] BiliBili YouTube
      The word "饿" (È - hungry) comes from the death sound "呃" (È - Uh/Eck) in the game 和平精英 (Game for Peace/PUBG Mobile). Meme Analysis.
